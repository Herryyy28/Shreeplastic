Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we handle your personal information and quotation files.

1. Introduction

At Shree Plastic Machinery, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers, website visitors, and business partners. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, store, and share information when you visit our website, use our inquiry forms, or interact with our sales and support representatives.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide high-quality services, respond to inquiries, and process quotation requests. We collect two types of information:

  • Personal Information: Contact person name, company name, email address, phone number, physical address, city, country, and any files (drawings, PDFs, specifications) uploaded through our quotation or contact forms.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device details, operating system, and browsing activity collected automatically via cookies and analytics software.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for standard business operations, including:

  • Reviewing engineering drawings and technical requirements to formulate custom platen and machinery quotations.
  • Communicating with you regarding sales agreements, service schedules, technical support, and customer satisfaction surveys.
  • Improving our website performance, layout, and visual presentation based on anonymized user traffic analysis.
  • Complying with necessary legal and compliance obligations relevant to global manufacturing and export operations.

4. Information Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal data against accidental loss, unauthorized access, altercations, or exposure. All data submitted via our contact and RFQ forms is transmitted over secure SSL encryption channels. Uploaded files (engineering drawings, specifications) are stored in secure directories accessible only to authorized technicians and sales engineers.

5. Information Sharing & Third Parties

Shree Plastic Machinery does not sell, rent, trade, or distribute your personal information to third-party marketing companies. We may share necessary details with trusted partners who assist us in operating our business under strict confidentiality agreements, such as:

  • Authorized regional sales representatives or agents who handle local installation and commissioning support.
  • Shipping and freight forwarders responsible for delivery operations of machinery to your facility.
  • Direct integration services like EmailJS and WhatsApp used to immediately notify our team about your urgent quotation inquiries.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website utilizes cookies and similar tracking tools to optimize navigation flow and analyze visitor demographics. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, though doing so might affect certain dynamic functionalities of the website.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have specific rights regarding your personal information, including the right to request access, correction, update, or deletion of the data we hold. Please contact us directly using the email listed below to exercise these rights.
