About Shree Plastic

Engineering Trust,
Mold by Mold

A dedicated B2B manufacturer delivering high-performance molding systems that maximize production capacity and minimize environmental impact across 60+ countries.

Our Mission

To empower manufacturers with high-efficiency, reliable injection molding machines that maximize output quality, reduce cycle times, and support sustainable production standards globally.

Our Vision

To be the globally preferred partner for high-precision molding technology, recognized for engineering innovation, customer-centric support, and green manufacturing practices.

Production Facility

State-of-the-Art
18,000 m² Facility

Our core manufacturing plant uses robotic welding, Japanese CNC machining centers, and heavy-capacity assembly cranes to construct platen frames with structural deflection minimization.

Every machine undergoes a rigorous 72-hour continuous load test, dry cycle calibration, and hydraulic pressure validation before dispatch.
